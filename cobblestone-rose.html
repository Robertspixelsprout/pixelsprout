About the Shop

Karen's corner of
downtown Saline.

The Cobblestone Rose is a one-of-a-kind boutique nestled in the heart of downtown Saline. Owner Karen has built something truly special — a shop where every item is chosen with purpose, and every visit feels like a discovery.

From exclusive home décor and handcrafted florals to gorgeous jewelry and boutique clothing, The Cobblestone Rose carries the kind of items you simply won't find anywhere else. If you can imagine it, Karen can probably source it or make it herself.

Stop in and see why locals call it their favorite shop in Saline. Special orders are always welcome.
